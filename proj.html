Welcome to JOTHI COIR COMPANY

The Jothi Coir Products has been operating for more than Ten years in the distribution of Coco Peat products for Agricultural and Horticultural use.

About Us

Jothi Coir Products was founded by Mr.R.Balu, initially started his business carrier in 2015, by purchasing the raw materials from the fiber Industries, Processing the raw materials by washing, drying and graded according to the customer requirements and outsourced to coco peat companies. The Technique of procurement, processing the raw materials & finally producing the quality product was the knowledge transition given by the experience in the coconut trading to our founder.

Later around 2019 Mr.R.Velayutham with his hands on experience and knowledge acquired from the Industries, decided to establish his own company to deliver quality and the most reliable coco peat products to his valuable customers. This laid the foundation for Jothi Coir Products in the year 2019 with a single 5 Kg Machine in a leased company.

The main strength of Jothi Coir Products is that we have total control over the entire process from sourcing the material from Jothi Coir Products own drying yards until making the end product. All this is done in-house, there by fulfilling the commitments in quality aspects and giving our customers very competitive prices, which would benefit the end-consumer.
